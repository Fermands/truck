ABOUT US

01 OVERVIEW

RELIABLE NATIONWIDE DRY VAN FREIGHT

Big Black Eagle (BBE) is a U.S. dry van carrier focused on reliable drop-and-hook operations and consistent freight movement across major U.S. lanes. Our operations combine experienced drivers, disciplined dispatch, and a safety-first culture to deliver dependable transportation every day.

We believe strong transportation companies are built by strong drivers. That's why we continue growing our fleet and welcome professional drivers who value reliability, safety, and long-term opportunities on the road.
